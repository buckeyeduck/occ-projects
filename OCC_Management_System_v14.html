⚠️ Route 3 — Churn Alert
Route 3 lost 366 stops and $17,632 in revenue year-over-year — a 12.7% stop decline vs. +4–38% growth on all other routes. This is the single largest risk in the business. Possible causes: driver/service quality issue on that route, geographic concentration in a neighborhood that moved to a competitor, or pricing sensitivity in that zip code cluster. Recommended action: Pull the specific addresses that dropped off Route 3 and look for a geographic cluster or common cancellation reason.
